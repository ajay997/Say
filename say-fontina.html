Our Story

Where Italian
Soul Meets
Noida's Heart

Nestled in the heart of Gulshan One29 Mall, Say Fontina is Noida's premier destination for authentic Italian and Mediterranean fine dining. Our culinary philosophy is rooted in the age-old traditions of Naples — where pizza is sacred, pasta is poetry, and every meal is a celebration.

Helmed by the celebrated Chef Bhasin, our kitchen crafts each dish with imported Italian ingredients, artisan techniques, and an unwavering passion for flavour. From our wood-fired Naples-style pizzas to handcrafted pastas and exquisite desserts — every plate tells a story.
